Lab vision and work

Our lab studies how microbes respond to environmental change, using physiology, genomics, and geochemical measurements to connect laboratory findings with real-world ecosystems.

We train students in quantitative microbial ecology, develop open tools, and collaborate across disciplines to advance research in microbial adaptation and resilience.
